Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a meal?

Open the app, tap the scan button and take a photo of your meal. The AI will automatically analyze the ingredients and nutritional values.

How do I connect Apple Health?

On first use, Lyna will ask for permission to access your health data. You can change these permissions in Settings > Health > Data Access > Lyna.

How do I delete my account?

Send an email to mehdi.aberkane.pro@gmail.com with your deletion request. We will process your request within 48 hours.

Is the app free?

Lyna offers a free version with basic features. A premium subscription is available to access all advanced features.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription in iPhone Settings > your name > Subscriptions > Lyna.
