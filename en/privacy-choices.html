Privacy Choices

Last updated: April 6, 2025

At Lyna, you stay in control of your data. Here are the choices you have regarding the collection and use of your information.

HealthKit Data

You can grant or revoke Lyna's access to your Apple Health data at any time.

How to: iPhone Settings > Health > Data Access > Lyna

Notifications

You can enable or disable Lyna's push notifications.

How to: iPhone Settings > Notifications > Lyna

Camera Access

Camera access is required to scan your meals. You can revoke it at any time.

How to: iPhone Settings > Lyna > Camera

Account Deletion

You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data.

How to: Contact us at mehdi.aberkane.pro@gmail.com

Access and Portability

You have the right to request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

How to: Contact us at mehdi.aberkane.pro@gmail.com

Contact

For any questions about your privacy choices, contact us at mehdi.aberkane.pro@gmail.com.
